About SYC Counselling

SYC Counselling believes in providing therapeutic counselling services that are easily accessible and affordable for individuals in need of support anywhere in Australia. Telehealth options of phone or online video counselling is one of the best ways for our services to reach those who may find it challenging to access therapeutic support in-person.

When it comes to your mental health and wellbeing, your treatment should be tailored to your needs. It is important to have your wants and needs heard and to go at the pace that you are ready to move forward with. Whether you just need a safe space to talk about things you cannot open up about to anyone else, or if you have counselling goals you would like to work towards together with your therapeutic counsellor.

A Therapeutic Counsellor works to help guide individuals towards improving general wellbeing and satisfaction with life, managing thoughts, learning new skills, and developing coping strategies to help better emotion regulation.
