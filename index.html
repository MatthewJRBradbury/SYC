About SYC Counselling

SYC Counselling provides therapeutic counselling services that are both affordable and accessible for individuals in need of support anywhere in Australia. We accomplish this via Telehealth options such as phone and online video counselling, which are some of the best ways for our services to reach those who may find it challenging to access therapeutic support in person.

When it comes to your mental health and wellbeing, SYC Counselling will tailor sessions to your needs so we can move in a particular way and pace that you are most comfortable with. Whether you just need a safe space to talk about things you cannot open up about to anyone else, or if you have counselling goals you would like to work towards together, reach out today as we would love to help!

How SYC Can Help

At SYC Counselling we will work to help guide you towards improving general wellbeing and satisfaction with life, managing thoughts, learning new skills, and developing coping strategies to help regulate emotions.
