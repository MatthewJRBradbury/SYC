About Me

Therapeutic Counseller

Sochetta Chhim

Phonetic: soh-chet-uh - ch-im

Therapeutic Counsellor registered with the Australian Counselling Association (ACA). Qualified with a Bachelor Degree of Social Science (Psychology), Graduate Diploma of Professional Psychology, and a Masters Degree of Counselling.

I was born and raised in Melbourne, Australia to my South East Asian parents. I grew up in two different cultures and am able to communicate in both English and Khmer (Cambodian language).

My passion for mental health began when I was in my last years of highschool. I had family and friends who all had their stories and challenges with everyday life and I always wanted to help somehow, so I learned the skills and knowledge needed to help support people who needed it

I began my counselling career working in a school, a community health group, and a private practice. I was able to experience working with people from all walks of life.

The best thing about working in mental health is that I get to learn new things all the time which further enables me to help people through their personal journey.
