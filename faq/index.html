FAQs

How do I book an appointment?

New Clients:

As a new client, you will need to contact us to book your first session.

Existing Clients:

Existing clients can book appointments by either contacting us directly or utilising our Online Booking option.

Do you have a cancellation fee?

Yes we do, if an appointment is not cancelled at least 24 hours prior to appointment time a fee of up to 50% of the appointment cost may be charged.

How should I prepare for my session?

Since our service is Telehealth only, it is recommended to make sure you have access to a quiet and private space at the time of your appointment. Access to a phone with reliable reception or a device with a reliable internet connection for video sessions is also a must.

Do you provide crisis services?

At SYC Counselling we do not provide crisis care, if you need to speak with someone urgently please call one of the following free 24/7 operated counselling services:

  • Lifeline on 13 11 14

  • Kids Helpline on 1800 55 1800

For Immediate emergencys, please call 000.

For Support with domestic / family violence or sexual assault please contact local law enforcement or contact 1800RESPECT on 1800 737 732.

Do you offer Medicare rebates?

Unfortunetly not, Counsellor’s are currently not eligible for Medicare rebates. The good news however is that our fees are already competitive to those offering Medicare rebates!
